# Marketing Beats Plan

## 🎯 Goal
Have **loyal players before release**

---

## 🧠 Core Concept

Marketing =

Visibility → Communication → Emotion → Offer

---

## 🔑 Key Message

"Cozy decorating game with cute animal clients"

Variants:
- "decorate rooms for adorable animals 🐷"
- "tiny cozy game about helping animals"
- "which room would you choose?"

---

## 👥 Target Audience

### Who?
- cozy gamers
- Animal Crossing fans
- Sims / decoration players
- casual / mobile players

### Where?
- YouTube Shorts
- itch.io
- Discord communities

### What they want?
- relaxing gameplay
- cute visuals
- simple mechanics
- emotional connection

---

## 📍 Channels

### Paid
- TikTok ads (optional)
- influencer integrations

### Organic (MAIN)
- TikTok
- YouTube Shorts
- Reddit (cozy / indie)
- Discord
- itch.io

---

## 🎥 Content Strategy

### Format rules:
- 5–10 seconds
- no explanation
- instant hook

---

### 🎬 Content ideas

1. "New client arrived 🐷"
2. "Before / After room"
3. "Which room would you choose?"
4. "Making cozy space for 🐶"
5. "Tiny game dev progress"

---

## 🔥 Marketing Beats (weekly)

### Every week:

- 2–4 TikToks
- 1 YouTube Short
- 1 GIF (VERY IMPORTANT)
- 1 devlog post

---

## 🧪 Iteration Loop

1. Post content
2. See what works
3. Double down
4. Kill weak ideas

---

## ⚠️ Failure Modes

- posting too rarely
- no clear message
- too complex videos
- no emotional hook
- no consistency

---

## 🏆 Rule

👉 Marketing beats everything

Even small games win with:
- consistency
- strong visuals
- right audience
