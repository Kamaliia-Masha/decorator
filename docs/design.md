# Decorator 

## 📖 About the Project

Decorator is a cozy interior decoration game where creativity meets design. You play as an interior designer who receives already furnished room templates from residents and redesigns them according to their requests.

Each resident arrives with their own space — a small apartment or an unusual micro-house — and a design brief describing required changes and the desired atmosphere.

Your role is to edit the existing layout: rearrange furniture, replace items, add new elements, and shape the overall mood of the room. The focus is on composition, visual harmony, and completing satisfying redesign commissions.

# 🔄 Core Gameplay Loop

## ➡️ ACTION

What the player does:

- Accept a design commission from a resident  
- Read the design brief  
- Edit the pre-made room template by:
  - Moving existing furniture  
  - Removing unwanted items  
  - Adding new furniture  
  - Replacing items with different variations  
- Arrange the room to match the requested mood and requirements  
- Press the **Submit** button  

---

## ➡️ REWARD

What the player receives after submission:

- A reaction message from the resident  
- In-game currency  
- Visual confirmation that the commission is completed  

The reward amount may depend on:
- Completion of required add/remove/replace tasks  
- Matching the requested mood  

---

## ➡️ EXPANSION

How the game progresses:

Currency can be used to unlock:

- New room templates  
- Additional floors or larger apartments  
- New furniture variations  
- Themed furniture sets (Forest, Vintage, Modern, etc.)  

This unlocks new commissions and increases room complexity, continuing the loop.

---

# 🧩 Basic 4 — MVP 

The minimal working version of the game must include:

### One Room
- One fully prepared room template  
- 5–7 furniture objects 

### One Commission
- One resident
- Clear add / remove / replace instructions  

### Core Interactions
- Move furniture (drag & drop)  
- Remove furniture  
- Add furniture from a panel  
- Replace one item with another  

### Basic Systems
- Submission button  
- Simple resident reaction message  
- Fixed currency reward  

---

# 🌟 Full-44

The expanded version builds on the Basic 4 and adds depth, variety, and progression.

### Multiple Rooms
- At least 3 different room templates  
- Different layouts and sizes  
- Themed environments  

### Multiple Commissions
- Several residents  
- Different design briefs  
- Increasing complexity  

### Expanded Furniture System
- Multiple variations per furniture category  
- Themed furniture sets (Forest, Vintage, Modern, etc.)

### Economy & Progression
- Unlockable:
  - New rooms  
  - Additional floors  
  - Furniture sets  

### Improved User Experience
- Room selection screen   
- Unlock progression screen  
- Different currency reward
